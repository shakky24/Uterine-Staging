Understanding Fibroids

What Are Uterine Fibroids?

Uterine fibroids are non-cancerous growths in the uterus that commonly affect women during reproductive age and can significantly impact quality of life.

Common Symptoms

  • ✓
    Heavy menstrual bleeding
  • ✓
    Pelvic pain or pressure
  • ✓
    Abdominal bloating
  • ✓
    Frequent urination
  • ✓
    Fertility concerns

When To Seek Help

If symptoms are affecting work, sleep, energy levels, daily comfort, or family planning, a specialist evaluation is worth doing early.

Best Non-Surgical Treatment

Uterine Artery Embolisation In Bangalore - Why Choose UAE?

UAE is one of the most advanced uterus-preserving treatments available for women looking to avoid surgery for fibroids.

How UAE Works

Image-guided, minimally invasive treatment

1 day stay

A tiny catheter is introduced through the wrist or groin to reach the uterine arteries, and the blood supply feeding the fibroids is blocked so they gradually shrink.

  • ✓
    Blood supply to fibroids is blocked
  • ✓

  • ✓

The Alternative To Surgery

Why Is UAE Easier Than Traditional Surgery?

Instead of removing the uterus or cutting fibroids surgically, UAE treats them from inside by blocking their blood supply.
