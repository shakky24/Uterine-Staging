Understanding Fibroids

What Are Uterine Fibroids?

Uterine fibroids are non-cancerous growths in the uterus that commonly affect women during reproductive age and can significantly impact quality of life.

Common Symptoms

  • ✓
    Heavy menstrual bleeding
  • ✓
    Pelvic pain or pressure
  • ✓
    Abdominal bloating
  • ✓
    Frequent urination
  • ✓
    Fertility concerns

When To Seek Help

If symptoms are affecting work, sleep, energy levels, daily comfort, or family planning, a specialist evaluation is worth doing early.

The Alternative To Surgery

Why Is UFE Easier Than Traditional Surgery?

Instead of removing the uterus or cutting fibroids surgically, UFE treats them from inside by blocking their blood supply.
